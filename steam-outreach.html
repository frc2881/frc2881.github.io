STEAM Outreach

Girl Scout Programs
​A core piece of The Lady Cans outreach is hosting Girl Scout STEAM badge workshops and summer camps. These badges are designed to introduce girls to STEAM fields, provide hands-on learning experiences, and encourage them to pursue their interests in STEAM.

FIRST Support

A second part of The Lady Cans outreach is supporting other FIRST teams and helping to grow FIRST programs in our local community. As a team, we are committed to helping spread the message of FIRST and giving back to the FIRST network as generously as we can.

Community Engagement
We run robot demonstrations in our community at local schools and businesses as a third portion of our outreach efforts. Our team uses these events to give people a close-up look at our robots in action, showcasing some of the unique STEAM opportunities the FIRST program offers.

Team Initiatives

Equity and inclusion are foundational principles of The Lady Cans. Our team initiatives take many forms, but the broad goal is to help shift mindsets, behaviors, and practices to promote an overall culture of respect and open-mindedness within STEAM.
