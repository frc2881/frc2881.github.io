Our Impact

​“I alone cannot change the world, but I can cast a stone across the waters to create many ripples.”
​
​— Mother Teresa

Ripples to Waves

The Lady Cans believe that shaping the future – and gender diversity – of STEAM starts with individuals. The ripple effect is a powerful phenomenon. A pebble thrown into a pond creates ripples that eventually become waves.
EMPOWER
teammates to develop STEAM skills
INSPIRE
other females to pursue STEAM
ADVOCATE
for gender diversity and equity in STEAM  ​
TRANSFORM
STEAM with new perspectives
